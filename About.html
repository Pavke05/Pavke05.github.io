Hibernians Basketball Club
Building champions on and off the court since 1984

Our Story

Hibernians Basketball Club was established in 1984 with the goal of promoting basketball in Malta. Over the years, we have become one of the most respected and successful teams in the country, winning multiple championships and fostering young talent.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To inspire, educate, and develop basketball players of all ages while promoting sportsmanship and excellence.

Vision: To become a leader in Maltese basketball and inspire the next generation of players to dream big and achieve greatness.

Our Achievements

  • Winners of the Maltese Basketball League: 2010, 2015, 2018
  • Development Program Award for Junior Teams: 2019
  • Produced 10 national-level players
