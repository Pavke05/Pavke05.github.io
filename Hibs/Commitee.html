Meet Our Committee

Our dedicated committee works tirelessly to drive the success of Hibernians Basketball Club. Get to know the people behind the scenes who make it all happen!

Ronnie Calleja

Role: President

Ronnie has been with the club for over 15 years, leading the team with a passion for basketball and community development. As President, he ensures the smooth operation of all activities within the club.

Melvin Buhagiar

Role: Vice President

Melvin has played a key role in developing the club's strategies and expanding its reach within the community. As Vice President, he supports the President and coordinates with various team managers.

Mario Tabone

Role: Treasurer

Mario is responsible for managing the club’s finances. He ensures all budgeting and financial decisions are made responsibly, keeping the club in good financial standing.

Anthony Cassar

Role: Secretary

Anthony is the backbone of the club’s administrative operations. He keeps track of important documents, organizes meetings, and ensures all communications are handled efficiently.

Darryl Farrugia

Role: Marketing Manager

Darryl oversees all marketing efforts for the club, from social media campaigns to local promotions. He helps raise the club’s profile and builds its relationship with fans and sponsors.

Winnie Buhagiar

Role: Event Coordinator

Winnie manages all events, from game day arrangements to social functions. She ensures everything runs smoothly and that all members and fans have a great experience.
